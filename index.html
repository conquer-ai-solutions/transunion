About This POC

Fraud Signal Triage Agent — Conquer AI × TransUnion

What This Demonstrates

This proof of concept shows the full confirm-or-correct workflow Conquer AI would build for TransUnion's fraud operations. AI classifies incoming fraud alerts, analysts review and confirm or override, and every decision logs to a full audit trail for regulatory review.

8 fraud alerts pre-loaded across 5 alert types
AI classification with confidence score, rationale, and suggested action
One-click confirm or structured correction with reason capture
Full audit trail — every decision logged and exportable as CSV

Alert Types in Queue

Five fraud patterns representative of TransUnion's environment:

Card Not Present Fraud — geographic mismatch, high IP risk, new device
Account Takeover — credential compromise, MFA bypass, unusual location
Synthetic Identity Fraud — thin file, SSN anomalies, failed verification
Identity Verification Failure — liveness check fail, low face match
First Party Fraud — high dispute velocity, chargeback pattern

Autonomy Level

This POC runs at Level 2 — Augment. AI classifies and recommends. Human confirms or corrects. No autonomous action without analyst sign-off, except explicit auto-routing of very high confidence, low false-positive alerts. Level 3 is the next phase.

Correct Classification

Override the AI's assessment. Your reason is logged as a training signal and improves future accuracy.
