About Fraud Signal Triage

AI-powered fraud alert classification and analyst review system

What This Tool Does

This fraud signal triage system uses AI to classify incoming fraud alerts and supports a confirm-or-correct workflow for analyst review. Every classification decision is logged to a complete audit trail for regulatory compliance and model improvement.

Real-time alerting — 8 sample fraud alerts across 5 alert types
AI classification — confidence scores, rationale, and recommended actions
Analyst workflow — one-click confirm or structured correction with reasoning
Audit trail — full record of every decision, exportable as CSV

Signal Types

Five fraud patterns representative of payment fraud environments:

Card Not Present Fraud — geographic mismatch, high IP risk, new device, velocity spikes
Account Takeover — credential compromise, MFA bypass, unusual location, rapid transaction
Synthetic Identity Fraud — thin credit file, SSN anomalies, failed verification
Identity Verification Failure — liveness check fail, low face match score
First Party Fraud — high dispute velocity, chargeback pattern, deliberate claims

AI Autonomy Level

This system operates at Level 2 — Augment. AI classifies and recommends actions. Human analysts confirm or override. Automatic resolution only for very high confidence, low false-positive-risk alerts.

Correct Classification

Override the AI assessment. Your correction is logged as a training signal and improves future accuracy.
