About This POC

Conquer AI's Proof of Concept for TransUnion's Fraud Signal Triage Agent. Demonstrates the confirm-or-correct workflow — AI classifies fraud alerts, analysts confirm or override, every decision logs to a full audit trail.

8 sample alerts pre-loaded across 5 fraud types. Model: claude-sonnet-4-20250514

How It Works

1. Alert Queue — incoming signals sorted by priority.

2. AI Classification — click any alert, hit Classify with AI. Claude returns risk type, confidence, rationale, and suggested action.

3. Confirm or Correct — confirm the AI is right, or override with your own classification and reason.

4. Audit Trail — every decision logged. Exportable as CSV.

Correct Classification

Override the AI's classification. Your correction is logged as a training signal.
