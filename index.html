About This POC

This is Conquer AI's Proof of Concept for TransUnion's Fraud Signal Triage Agent. It demonstrates the confirm-or-correct workflow: AI classifies incoming fraud alerts, analysts confirm or correct the classification, and every decision is logged to a full audit trail.

8 sample alerts are pre-loaded across 5 fraud types: Card Not Present, Account Takeover, Synthetic Identity, Identity Verification Failure, and First Party Fraud.

Model: claude-sonnet-4-20250514

How It Works

1. Alert Queue — incoming signals from fraud detection engines, sorted by priority.

2. AI Classification — click any alert, then hit "Classify with AI". Claude analyses all signal fields and returns a risk type, urgency, confidence score, rationale, and suggested action.

3. Confirm or Correct — you confirm the AI is right, or override with your own classification. The reason you provide becomes a training signal.

4. Audit Trail — every decision is logged with full detail. Exportable as CSV for regulatory review.

Correct Classification

Override the AI's classification. Your correction is logged as a training signal and will improve future accuracy.
